WELCOME TO ANSEL

Ansel Education Consultancy was established & registered in 2017 in UK. Later a branch office was also registered in India for marketing purposes. We have also a good network of referrals all over South India and other countries as well. We have experienced staffs to support all student services from counselling to visa process.

Our main purpose is to advise students on their chosen studies in their chosen country. We recruit students to various institutions across the world through our friendly and efficient services. Our service is attracted by many students.

We act in between the institutions and students to make admission process smoothly and the visa process successfully.

We are British council certified, ICEF certified, Education New Zealand trained agent and Cambridge English Language assessed.

We have grown rapidly in these years. Parents and students were satisfied with our service. We also train students with free materials for IELTS.

We also support Indian students on education and maintenance loans

We also help students on finding part time job, internships etc in UK only
